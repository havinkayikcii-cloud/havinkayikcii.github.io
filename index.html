About

I'm Havin — a writer at the intersection of economics, finance, and the way young people actually experience money and markets.

Most finance writing is either too academic to be useful or too shallow to be interesting. I sit somewhere in the middle: research-backed, critical analysis that reads like a conversation, not a textbook.

I write for companies and publications that want to reach audiences who are smart, skeptical, and tired of being talked down to.
